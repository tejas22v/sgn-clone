Infrastructure

Shanti Gyan Niketan School boasts of the 'state-of-the-art' infrastructure, providing the students with best Laboratories (computer, physics, chemistry, biology and maths) equipped with the latest instruments and apparatus. Art rooms are artistically and aesthetically designed to cater the needs of children. The library has a rich collection of books for both teacher and the taught to instill the habit of reading. The school boasts of a music room which creates a conducive environment for the learning of the music notes and dance steps. School also provides a well-equipped infirmary with a qualified nurse.
